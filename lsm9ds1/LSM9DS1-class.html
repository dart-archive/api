Accelerometer, gyroscope and magnetometer.

Constructors

LSM9DS1({accelGyroDevice, magnetDevice})

The two arguments are the I2C device for the accelerometer and gyroscope and the I2C device for the magnetometer. If either of these are null that part of the chip will not be initialized.
